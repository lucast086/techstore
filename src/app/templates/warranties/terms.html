Standard Warranty Coverage

Coverage Period

  • Parts: 90 days from delivery date
  • Labor: 30 days from delivery date
  • Screen Repairs: 7 days from delivery date
  • Software Services: No warranty coverage

What's Covered

  • Defects in parts replaced during the original repair
  • Workmanship issues related to the original repair
  • Failure of repaired components under normal use conditions
  • Issues directly related to the original repair problem

What's NOT Covered

  • Physical damage caused after the repair (drops, spills, etc.)
  • Water damage or liquid exposure
  • Unauthorized modifications or repairs
  • Software issues not related to hardware repairs
  • Cosmetic damage that doesn't affect functionality
  • Issues caused by third-party accessories
  • Normal wear and tear
  • Data loss or recovery

Warranty Claim Process

  1. Contact the store within the warranty period
  2. Provide warranty number or original repair receipt
  3. Describe the issue you're experiencing
  4. Bring the device for inspection
  5. If approved, repairs will be performed at no charge
  6. Warranty repairs typically completed within 48 hours

Important Conditions

  • Warranty is non-transferable and applies only to the original customer
  • Proof of original repair required for all warranty claims
  • Warranty void if device shows signs of tampering or unauthorized repair
  • Decision on warranty coverage is at the discretion of our technicians
  • Warranty does not extend the original warranty period

Extended Warranty Options

Extended warranty plans are available for purchase at the time of repair completion. Ask our staff for details about extended coverage options.

Last Updated: January 2024
These terms are subject to change. The warranty terms applicable to your repair are those in effect at the time of your original repair service.
